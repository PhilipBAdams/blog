Bio

I am a software engineer at Waymo. Previously, I worked on Bing. Before that, I was a Math/CS student at UChicago. I’m primarily interested in systems that help us process and understand unstructured data, in particular search and example mining.

I’m also interested in aviation, photography, functional programming, and backpacking. I may touch on some of these topics on this blog, and you can see some of my photos here.

Contact
